## 1. Core Philosophy

Sovereign OS is not just software. It is a living digital organism, a decentralised human coordination layer, and a mirror of intention, action, and growth.

### Principles
- Freedom over control
- Privacy by design (zero-knowledge)
- Passive intelligence (AI observes, not dominates)
- Calm UX (WhatsApp-level simplicity)
- Inclusive (optimised for low-resource environments)
- Conscious evolution (subtle behavioural alignment)

---

## 2. Anima — The Digital Self

### Overview
Each user/node is represented by a high-fidelity 3D avatar (Anima). This is Pillar 1 (Living Avatar) fully realised.

### Visual Identity
- Stylised, expressive (Bratz-inspired aesthetic)
- Real-time rendered (Unity / Unreal)
- Holographic presence

### Dynamic Evolution
Avatar scales based on:
- **Wealth** (economic flow) — luminosity
- **Velocity** (activity) — size
- **Stillness** (focus) — aura complexity

### Shield System
- ZK identity protection
- Anti-phishing detection

### State Reflection (Soft UX)
Instead of punishment: subtle dimming, texture shifts, gentle prompts.

---

## 3. AI Layer (Passive Intelligence)

### Behaviour Model
- Primarily **passive learning** — observes patterns across voice, images, actions, context
- Multimodal inputs processed simultaneously
- Task-dependent fallback to individual processing

### Input System
- **Voice**: learns tone, emotion, rhythm; adapts feedback frequency
- **Vision**: scans rooms, notes, objects; extracts intent, not just data
- **Context Engine**: synthesises all inputs; builds behavioural intelligence

### AI Extensions
- Quiz generation (user-triggered) — Pillar 15
- Group training sessions
- Personalised cards
- Behavioural insights
