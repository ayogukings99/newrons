🔒 Supply Chain

Trustless escrow for African trade.

Lock funds on-chain. Both parties sign to release. No bank. No middleman. Just math.

  • Dual-signature release — buyer and supplier both sign. Funds move only when both agree.
  • Multi-currency — trade in NXT locally or USDC on Solana for cross-border deals.
  • Arbiter safety net — disputes go to a neutral newrons arbiter, resolved in 48 hours.
  • On-chain proof — every settlement is a Solana transaction, publicly verifiable forever.
  • PO-linked — every escrow maps to a real Purchase Order in your supply chain.

🪙 NXT Token

Value from contribution, not speculation.

NXT is earned through real economic activity — trade, knowledge, community service. It is the unit of account for the African cooperative economy.

  • Earned, not bought — NXT flows from real work and real contribution.
  • Community-backed — each NXT unit is a claim on community value, not thin air.
  • Cross-border ready — bridge to USDC on Solana for international settlement.
  • Non-speculative by design — stability mechanisms prevent pump-and-dump dynamics.
